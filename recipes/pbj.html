Peanut butter & jelly sandwitch

This is an old classic recipe with a small twist. Instead of your a bog-standard PBJ sandwitch, we add (and remove) some stuff that my grandpa discovered in World-War 2 when a guard, who slipped on a Camel® cigarette butt & fell down from the guard tower, had a small nut and some butter on his person.

Ingredients

  1. hard work or clean hands
  2. 1 pea-sized nut (required!)
  3. some butter (margarine works too, but only if its from Country Crock)
  4. some blood and tears (makes it more fulfilling)
  5. bread (optional)
